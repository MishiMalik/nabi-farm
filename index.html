NABI FARMS LIMITED

Delivering eco-friendly farm produce for all

Nabi Farms LTD (NFL) modern agricultural extensive enterprise located in Pap-Onditi, Kisumu County, Kenya. Founded in 2020, the farm is registered as a private limited company in Kenya. The focus areas of the farm include arable crops, horticulture, aquaculture, poultry and livestock production. NFL practices integrated farming system towards sustainable agriculture. Nabi Farms utilizes modern green technologies to mitigate the effects of climate change.

ABOUT NABI FARMS

Nabi Farms LTD is a professionally run enterprise. It is located within western Kenya in Kisumu County. The farm pride itself in being well-networked with long-standing partners within Kenya. Our robust supply chain management systems allow us to procure and deliver produce and commodities promptly.

The farm serves as a resource center, conducting trainings and capacity building to communities towards economic empowerment and sustainable food security.
